For the map Susp Bool ‚Üí S¬π, we have to specify the behavior of two path constructors. The idea is to map one to loop and one to refl.

Proving that the two maps cancel each other requires some primitives of Cubical Agda that we won‚Äôt really discuss in the lectures, namely hcomp and hfill. These are used (among other things) to define path composition and ensure that it behaves correctly.
